Join the AID Lab — open positions for Ph.D. students and postdocs

→ Recruiting · Fall 2027

We are looking for PhD students with a background in physics, applied math, complex systems, or computer science — and a willingness to read papers from the others. Postdoc positions will open in 2027.

PhD students

Apply to UR Physics by December 15.

Apply to the University of Rochester Department of Physics & Astronomy Ph.D. program through the online application — the deadline is December 15, the GRE is optional, and a fee waiver is available if you need it. Name the AID Lab in your statement of purpose, and feel free to email Yuanzhao first with a paragraph about what you're curious about.

Postdocs

Open to applications now.

Yuanzhao welcomes postdoc inquiries on a rolling basis — now and going forward — in the Department of Physics & Astronomy. Email him a short note on what you'd like to work on, along with a CV. If you're applying for your own fellowship, he's glad to host and help shape the proposal; funded openings will be posted here as they come up.

UR undergraduates

Independent study and summer research.

Enrolled UR undergrads can join for independent-study credit during the year. For the summer, the university funds research through the Schwartz Discover Grant and the department's research opportunities — reach out and we'll figure out which fits.
